Nature & Landscapes

From misty mountains to pristine beaches

Vietnam's natural landscapes are among the most diverse and stunning in Southeast Asia. From the terraced rice fields of the north to the white sand beaches of the south, nature lovers will be in paradise.

Natural Wonders

Best Times to Visit

  • North Vietnam (Sapa, Ha Long): September to November for clear skies
  • Central Vietnam (Hoi An, Da Nang): February to May for dry weather
  • South Vietnam (HCMC, Mekong Delta): December to April for sunshine
  • Beaches: April to August for the best swimming conditions
